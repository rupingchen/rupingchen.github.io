100 Days of UX Writing

In the same vein as the 100 Day Design Challenge, I've decided to do 100 Days of UX Writing. Unlike design and all of its offshoots (e.g. interior design, UX, IxD), UX Writing has yet to find any sort of definition (i.e. nobody really knows what it is yet). So I'm doing a variety of projects: writing quirky copy for empty states, editing the Foreword in Uber's "77 Things," among other projects that I hope you'll find compelling.
